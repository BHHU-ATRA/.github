# Herpetological Association of Bosnia and Herzegovina (ATRA)

> Advancing research and conservation of amphibians, reptiles, and their habitats in Bosnia and Herzegovina.

## 🐸 About ATRA

The **Herpetological Association of Bosnia and Herzegovina** conducts scientific research and implements conservation projects focused on local herpetofauna species. See more on [bhhuatra.com](https://www.bhhuatra.com).
